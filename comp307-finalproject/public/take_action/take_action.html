Actionable Steps

Tips for Reducing Personal Carbon Footprints

  • Use public transportation, walk, or bike instead of driving.
  • Reduce, reuse, recycle.
  • Conserve energy at home by using energy-efficient appliances.
  • Support renewable energy sources.
  • Eat a plant-based diet to lower the carbon footprint of your food.
